Feel Satisfied.

By playing chess, not only do you learn important life skills, but you also are able to have fun. Nothing can give as much satisfaction as finding a brilliant skewer, mate, or tactic. Relive all these moments after the game with expert analysis making sure that the satisfaction would be a constant. In private lessons, the student is sure to learn and improve. This is a great opportunity for beginners, intermediate students, and advanced students to get a solid foundation.

Achieve Success.

These lessons are important as they help prepare you for future tournaments and events. During these lessons, Coach Emil will increase your understanding of the game so tat the next time you play in a competition, you would be sure to crush your opposition. Not only will you have fun, but you will also win, something that for International GM Emil Anka is not uncommon. After signing up for your classes, make sure to also take a look at ChessSport’s Special Events.

Learn Strategy.

Chess is a game filled with tactics and positional manouevers. Though all these ideas and themes seem to be unrelated they all fall under a common roof. They are all strategic ideas and they will all be taught in these classes. Additionally, Coach Emil offers a personalized approach to every students and makes sure that they can apply all of them in real-world situations. These lessons will help improve your students in-game and real-world thinking process. Sign up for group lessons below so as to not miss out on this oppurtunity.

Strategy. Satisfaction. Success.

Having earned a 4-year university degree in chess coaching, Grandmaster Anka has coached for over 30 years. He has played over 2000 tournament games, published dozens of chess-related articles, and has become a certified FIDE International Arbiter. Emil is a well-organized, patient, exciting, and successful chess coach who fosters the development of self-discipline, maturity, and consistency; skills that lead toward success in any endeavor. Coach Anka currently resides in Kirkland, WA and leads private lessons, after-school enrichment programs, and run several local chess tournaments.

Chess Lessons For All Levels!

To start off with Coach Emil offers a professional assessment to determine your level. Afterwards, personalized chess lessons are created to better help your student learn. Nowhere else can you find such high level game analysis, lesson topics, and organization. Lessons could be either in person or online and can be taught in English, French, German, or Hungarian. Sign up now to learn chess at the highest level.

ChessSport Special Events

Every year, ChessSports, led by International Fide Arbiter and Grandmaster, Emil Anka, hosts different chess events. Not only do they help increase your player’s rating, but it also helps their preparedness for future tournaments. For example, almost every week, a few months before nationals, ChessSport hosts National Prep where all his students play a game against each other and afterwards, Coach Emil will walk through and analyze all the games. Not only are the tournaments fun, but they are also helpful!
